About Us

MosaicMake is an idea created by a lecturer and passed onto 5 of his brightest and wisest to complete for a summer of learning at the Insight Center for Data Analytics, UCC, Ireland. Us, being the 5, would like to welcome you to MosaicMake and hope you enjoy this mosaic builder. Whether its to make a birthday collage, a celebration of an event or for your business or to mess about; we hope you take the opportunity to explore the many filters and photo finessing options available.

Many thanks from the team,
Alessia, Cathy, Claire, Eimear, Gaby.
